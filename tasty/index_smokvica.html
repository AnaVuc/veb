The Smokvica story began with four friends dream to open a restaurant that will be the favorite place for gathering their friends, families and all who choose to enjoy all their senses as often as possible.

***

The promise on which the philosophy of all the restaurants today is given, before the first Smokvica started to work – everything that is signed with the name Smokvica will bear the seal of authenticity and craft access.

***

The mission was also known from the very beginning – to serve the best food and drink, nothing less than that.




***

Today, with a large number of Smokvica restaurants all around Belgrade, which are completely adapted to the environment in which they are located, the intentions of the four friends has succeeded.
