***

Not a lot of people had such zest for life as the legendary Mexican artist Frida Kahlo. She welcomed each day with love and passion. She inspired people around her to look at life from different angles.

***

Her passion for life was an inspiration for bar restaurant Cantina de Frida. This is the place where friends meet to sing and dance together, and to share their meal, in praise of life.

***

Cantina de Frida menu is based on the numerous, diverse vivid and tasty meals from Mexico, South America, Spain and the rest of the Mediterranean in small plates (tapas).

***

They offer large plates too, with bigger portions for sharing with friends. All of the meals are made with the same idea: it`s important to enjoy every bite of the food.
